Job Market Trend

Employment of civil engineers is projected to grow 5 percent from 2022 to 2032, faster than the average for all occupations. About 21,200 openings for civil engineers are projected each year, on average, over the decade.

The civil engineering industry employs over 40 million people in India. Contributes 10% to India's GDP. Expected to grow due to government initiatives and demand for infrastructure. The construction industry is projected to grow at 5.6% CAGR during 2021-2025.
